This is where the journey starts

Classes are where the transformative powers of yoga, meditation and mindfulness are shown and learnt. They will teach you how to get in touch with your mind and body through technique, practice and dedication. Every class ensures that you learn more about your inner self and that you get one step closer to achieving harmony between body and mind.

No matter what your proficieny is in yoga, our classes are for everyone, because we believe that combining yoga and mindset offers powerful benefits that can significantly enhance your well being. There will always be a connection in yoga practices to the mindset as they are inextricably intertwined.

Yoga classes offered

Day

Afternoon

  • 11:00 - 12:30 pm: Mindset practice

  • Your free time to REST or Private Coaching; T.M Meditation (can be after dinner as well)

Evening

  • 16:00 - 17:15 pm: Karma Yoga practice
    of Flow, Vinyasa and Active Meditation varieties
  • 17:30 - 18:30 pm Mindset practice
  • 19:00 - 20:30 pm Dinner
