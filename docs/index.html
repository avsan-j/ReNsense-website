Common Myths about yourself

Positive thinking alone is enough.

Positive thinking is a powerful tool, but it is essential to pair it with actionable steps. Learn how to combine positive thinking with practical strategies to change your life.

Stress is always bad and failure is a sign to quit.

Not all stress is detrimental and failure can serve as a stepping stone to success. Discover the techniques to harness stress as a motivating force and find out how to reframe failures into learning experiences.

I'm too old for change


The mind can be reshaped at any age, with self awareness and effort. Explore the science of neuroplasticity and realize how small, consistent changes lead to monumental growth.

You can change your habits overnight

Creating lasting habits takes time and persistence. Explore the science of habit formation and learn how small, consistent changes lead to monumental growth.

If you feel anxious, you aren't meditating right

Anxiety during medidation is not uncommon, it's part of the process. Uncover techniques to navigate through anxiety and deepen your meditation practice effectively.

Success is determined by innate talent

While talent can play a role, success is more often the result of hard work and a growth mindset. Believing in the potential for improvement is crucial for achieving success.
