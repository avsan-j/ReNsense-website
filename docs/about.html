Namaste and Welcome!

I am thrilled that you are interested in learning more about this wonderful program and me.

Hi there!

My name is Dikla, and while I was born in Israel, I consider myself a global citizen. For the past 25 years, I have dedicated my life to supporting individuals on their personal journeys as a professional yoga instructor, certified yoga therapist, and mindset coach. At the age of fifteen, I successfully recovered from cancer, which solidified my understanding that my role in life is to guide people on their inner journey to healing through mind and body.

I am passionate about humanity. People are the most fascinating, complex, and exciting landscapes ever created.I cherish the unique symbiosis between the body, mind, and soul. I bring all this love and compassion into my sessions, along with rich and varied experience in different yoga practices, meditation techniques, and mindset methods. Drawing on my extensive knowledge and intuition from teaching thousands of yogis worldwide, I have created a unique blend of yoga practice and mindset coaching for deeper, harmonious, and sustainable change. I genuinely believe that this combination can create a powerful synergy that catalyzes significant improvements in the lives of those who desire a fulfilling and peaceful life.


Why choose me?

  • Deep Listening: I dive deeply into your life story and craft a personalized journey just for you.
  • Ancient Wisdom and Modern Approach: I blend ancient yoga wisdom with contemporary scientific understanding to bridge these worlds and offer you the best of both
  • Extensive Knowledge: Continuous training and research enable me to bring the latest innovations to our sessions.
  • Dedication and Commitment: I am profoundly committed to your progress and development, evident in every meeting and practice.

After many years of learning and teaching around the world, I have come to understand that there is only one thing that can heal all wounds: loving yourself. Amazingly, when people start loving themselves truly and deeply, their lives improve, bringing good health, abundance, profound joy, and inner peace. It's a simple premise: loving yourself. Life is a voyage of self-discovery, and to be enlightened means going within, shedding light on all you discover about yourself, and loving it, no matter what. That is all there is.

My ambition, hope, and dreams are to help everyone reach and exceed their best version of themselves. I humbly invite you to join our beautiful, diverse family of clients and experience the magic of discovering your inner strength, awakening to a life full of meaning, deep joy, and lasting peace.

This is your time to shine.

Mission

My mission is to open minds, uplift spirits and inspire action towards a better life for all. I aim to be a guide to others, helping them discover the best version of themselves. I wish to empower individuals to overcome obstacles and cultivate positive change in their lives.

At ReNsense, we believe that true happiness comes from within. Our mission is to help individuals grow personally through self-reflection and healing in a supportive and welcoming community. We host weekend retreats where participants discover practical tools and insights that fit seamlessly into daily life, helping them stay strong and resilient against challenges. We encourage self-belief and personal growth, showing everyone that they have the power to shape their journey. By combining wisdom and transformative experiences, we aim to create meaningful change and understanding in each person's life.
