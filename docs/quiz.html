Ask yourself: How much do I know?

Here are some terms below related to yoga and mindset. Try to find all the terms you know of, and no cheating, we are watching you as you take the quiz (not really, just kidding ;) ). If you dont know any of the terms, click or hover over them to know what they mean:

  • Insistence
    • Determined to doing something
  • Subconsciousness
    • Part of the brain operating beneath consciousness
  • Affirmation
    • Focusing on positive thoughts during yoga
  • Mindfulness
    • To focus on the action happening at the moment
  • Self Love
    • Showing respect and kindness to one's values
  • Resilience
    • Ability to adapt to stress and adversity
  • Inner Voice
    • Your innate wisdom, love and compassion
